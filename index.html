About JIMS


AICTE Approved | GGSIPU Affiliated

JIMS Engineering Management Technical Campus, Greater Noida

JIMS Engineering Management Technical Campus (JEMTEC) is one of the top GGSIPU affiliated colleges in Delhi/NCR. Located at 48/4, Knowledge Park III, Greater Noida, it offers state-of-the-art learning spaces with smart classrooms, well-equipped engineering labs, and extensive learning zones.

CareerChoice360 is our integrated career development platform that bridges education with real-world opportunities — internships, industry visits, corporate mentorship, and placement support all under one roof.

Wi-Fi Enabled Campus - High-speed internet in classrooms, library, labs & hostel

Smart Classrooms - AC classrooms with projectors and digital learning tools

Secured Campus - CCTV enabled with 24x7 security, hostel within campus premises
